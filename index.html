Touchless System
Demonstration

Our application utilizes computer vision to translate standard hand gestures into immediate system commands. By mapping physical movements to digital outputs, users can seamlessly control interfaces without physical contact.

Active Gesture Guide

☝️

Cursor Mode (Index finger up)

Moves your mouse cursor smoothly within an Active Control Region to reduce wrist strain.

👌

Single Click (Pinch + 3 fingers up)

A quick tap and release to left-click. The 3 back fingers act as a "safety switch" to prevent accidental misfires.

👌👌

Double Click (Double pinch)

Two quick taps with the thumb and index finger to execute a double-click command.

🤏

Drag & Drop (Hold pinch)

Hold the pinch for ~0.45 seconds to lock the mouse button down. Open the pinch to drop.

✌️

Scroll Mode (Index & Middle up)

Freezes the cursor in place. Moving your palm up or down infinitely scrolls the active window.

🤙

Screenshot (Shaka gesture)

Triggers a 3-second UI countdown, flashes the screen, and safely saves a screenshot to the disk.

✋

Frozen / Idle (Unknown gesture)

Instantly freezes the cursor and safely drops any active drags if the tracking is lost.
