Touchless System
Demonstration

Our application utilizes computer vision to translate standard hand gestures into immediate system commands. By mapping physical movements to digital outputs, users can seamlessly control interfaces without physical contact.

Active Gesture Guide

01

Wave Gesture (Wake / Hello)

As demonstrated by the mascot, raising and waving the right hand initializes the tracking system and wakes the application from standby mode.

02

Swipe Left / Right (Navigation)

Swiping a flat palm horizontally across the camera viewport allows touch-free scrolling through menus and presentation slides.
